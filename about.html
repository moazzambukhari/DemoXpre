Company Overview

Established in 1999, KalSoft has been a leader in technology-driven business solutions, delivering excellence and agility for over two decades. Our latest initiative, XPreCon, revolutionizes the pre-construction real estate industry by automating and streamlining processes. XPreCon provides real-time insights and enhances collaboration for developers, marketing agencies, brokerage houses, and realtors.

Partnered with industry leaders like AWS and VMware, we offer scalable and cost-effective solutions, leveraging the latest cloud and virtualization technologies. Our mission is to empower clients with innovative tools that transform real estate development, marketing, and sales, ensuring efficiency and productivity.

Experience XPreCon's transformative power. Contact us today to explore how our platform can drive innovation, efficiency, and success in your real estate projects.
